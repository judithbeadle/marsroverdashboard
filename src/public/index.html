Mars Explorer

The goal of the Mars Exploration Program is to explore Mars and to provide a continuous flow of scientific information and discovery through a carefully selected series of robotic orbiters, landers and mobile laboratories interconnected by a high-bandwidth Mars/Earth communications network.

Now you can join the mission and explore Mars yourself via real time rover data. Enter the dashboard and start gathering data using one of four rovers, two of which are currently active. Chose a rover, date and rover camera to gather image data and find out facts about each rover and their unique missions. Then go ahead and explore some more with Nasa...
